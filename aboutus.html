About Us

Andre Gilbert created Gestion Infogila Ltd in 1989. THis diversified corporation offered turnkey systems for administrative applications, including equipment, networks, off-the-shelf accounting software, in-house software (including rental) and all the accompanying services. It was the leading reseller of Accpac accounting products (largest in the world) for over 4 years in Canada. In 1997, Infogila divested all its operations, except for the Rental Software division and created Orion Software.

Orion developed Sirius, a new Windows 32-bit product,including multi-language, multi-tax and multi-currency capabilities. It released its English version in the fall of 2000 and is now developing the Latin American market.

Orion is the industry leader in Quebec, and the second largest supplier of rental software in Canada. Our team is composed of young and dynamic professionals (including several MBAs), backed with 15 years of practical experience serving the rental industry. We concentrate all our efforts towards a single objective: offering the finest Rental Software and complementary services in North America.

Finally, Orion Software is an active player in the rental industry. Member of the American Rental Association and the Canadian Rental Association, we participate in the yearly rental shows and monthly meetings, and naturally read the major rental magazines. This industry participation and customer feedback allows us to clearly understand the different challenges that the industry faces, and drives us to adapt our software and services to the changing needs of the rental centers.

Orion Software, your partner in technology!
